pomme dorée

Pomme dorée!! Wait, isn't it 'pomodoro' though?

So, yes, 'pomodoro' literally means 'tomato' in Italian. However, what fun is it to build a website for this technique of spaced, timed sessions of focus without a, presumably, witty word play? As I, Phillip Gallas, also speak french among other languages, I swear I am not trying to flex, (il faut que je dis plutôt que je suis en train d'apprendre la langue encore), I decided to search for a mixture of words that sound similar, hence 'la pomme dorée', the golden apple, came to be! I also like playing Minecraft sometimes, and the golden apple in the game came in handy for the logo. And how to use the website?
